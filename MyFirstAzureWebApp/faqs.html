Teleschool is an initiative by Ministry of Federal Education to digitalize the education of Pakistan. It is an app where you can view educational content for all grades from Kindergarten (KG) to 12. The content is available as a library and as 06 live channels.

The app is available on both Google Play Store and iOS App Store.

Content of all grades from KG to 12 is available on the app.

There are 06 channels on the app.

  • Nonehal for grades KG to 5
  • Hunehar for grades 6 to 8
  • Nojawan 1 for grades 9 and 10
  • Nojawan 2 for grades 11 and 12
  • Jald Seekho 1 for accelerated learning program for out of school students for grades 1 to 8
  • Jald Seekho 2 for accelerated learning program for out of school students for grades 9 to 12

You can also search your grade or subject in the search bar to view available content in the content library.
